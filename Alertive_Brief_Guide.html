Alertive

Brief Training Guide

Quick reference for getting started — for more information visit alertive.co.uk

← Back to menu

What Alertive Does.

  • Secure messaging app for healthcare teams — works on any device
  • Connects with hospital systems (telephony, patient records, pathology)
  • Sends critical alerts, tasks, and general chat messages
  • Tracks delivery, read receipts, and responses in real time

Getting Set Up.

  • Download "Alertive" from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android)
  • Allow critical notifications so emergency alerts can reach you
  • Set a 4-digit passcode; enable biometrics on personal devices
  • Enter your Organisation ID, then sign in with your Trust email and password

Starting Your Shift.

  • Enable "Fast Sign In" on personal devices for quick pin/biometric access
  • Your accounts screen shows your personal inbox plus any roles you hold
  • Always sign out fully on shared devices

Roles & Handovers.

  • A role represents a responsibility (e.g. "ED Senior Consultant") that can be shared across shifts
  • Acquire a role at the start of your shift so colleagues can reach you
  • Occupied roles show in purple with the current holder's name; empty roles are grey
  • At the end of your shift: Relinquish (leave empty) or Give (hand to a named colleague)
  • Use Take to pull an occupied role over to yourself if needed

Finding People.

  • Tap the avatar icon (bottom right) to open the Contact Directory
  • Filter by Users, Roles, or Groups
  • Star favourites for quick access; recent contacts appear automatically

Alerts.

  • Use alerts to flag something urgent — replaces traditional bleeps
  • Choose recipients and response options; track who's read and responded
  • Emergency alerts break through the lock screen with sound
  • Can include a title, overview, patient context, images and audio

Tasks.

  • For less time-critical, more detailed requests (e.g. discharge summary, x-ray request)
  • Can include priority, due date, and patient/location details
  • Tasks move through stages: accept, decline, in progress, blocked, done
  • Expired tasks show with a strike-through and can be deleted

Conversations.

  • Use instead of WhatsApp/text for work chats — keeps things secure and separate
  • Start a direct or group conversation from the create button or a contact card
  • Group conversations can be patient-specific and linked to EPR details
  • Send messages, voice notes, videos and attachments

Managing Your Inbox.

  • Colours and icons show what's in your inbox at a glance
  • Blue dot = unread
  • Use tabs and filters to narrow down your list
  • Pin important items to the top, or flag for later
  • Multi-select to mark items read/unread or delete in bulk

Settings & Support.

  • Update job title, contact number, and login preferences in Settings
  • Share feedback directly in the app
  • Invite colleagues to join Alertive
  • Access training materials and support via the Help section
